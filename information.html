Hagen Eberle and Matthias Holznagel, alias Fondation Tschuess, operate with a desire to show, share and assemble.
Through an artistic and scenographic approach, FT organizes exhibitions, happenings and screenings.

So far FT has kindly been supported by Kulturbüro Karlsruhe, Studienstiftung des deutschen Volkes, Karlsruher Fächer GmbH, Privatbrauerei Hoepfner, Jürgen Galli, Courtney Jaeger Basel, Galerie Meyer Riegger, By bye avondale 2850 N Pulaski Rd
